Overview

On the evening of 11.11.18, churches from across the city will gather under One name: Jesus.

We're not sponsored by any single church. We are a group of leaders from churches across the Austin area. Our goal is to see people healed, saved, and delivered and connected to a local church. This event is a service to the local churches of Austin by coming under the label of the UNITED Church of Austin.

Why should you get involved?

We want to honor you, as a leader and a voice in the Church of Austin. We're only here because of you and the work the Lord has called you to. You have an individual/unique sword with the word of the Lord engraved on it. We want you to raise it up and drive it in.

As part of the night, the leaders of the various areas of the greater Austin area will gather together to make declarations over the city. We believe that each corner of the City carries a unique piece of what the Lord wants to do in Austin. We will unite our voices and declare the goodness of the Lord over Austin!

How can you get involved?

There are several ways that you can help. If you're interested in partnering, we ask that you begin by filling out our partnership form. In addition, we have several other ways for your local body to get involved:

1. Donate - We are currently looking to raise $10,000 to cover the costs of hosting the event.

2. Volunteers - We have several areas that people are able to get involved and help with the event. We would love to partner with your local church body for areas like parking, greeting, and prayer. If your local body is able to help, indicate that on the partnership form when you fill it out and our team will be in touch with you.

3. Spread the word - We have listed several resources below to help get the word out for the event. Please fill free to pass this information along to other pastors in the area!
